Real Estate Law

Real estate transactions and disputes involve significant capital and high value assets. Our practice provides comprehensive legal support to developers, investors, and homeowners, ensuring that their property rights are secure and their transactions transparent.

We meticulously review titles, negotiate construction contracts, and resolve landlord-tenant disputes with efficiency. Our goal is to prevent litigation through careful drafting and due diligence, but we remain ready to litigate aggressively when property rights are challenged.

In a sector rife with complexity, we provide the clarity and security our clients need to make informed investment decisions.

Key Areas of Practice

  • Property Acquisition & Sale
  • Landlord-Tenant Disputes
  • Construction Contracts
  • Title Verification & Due Diligence
  • Real Estate Development
  • Lease Agreements
  • Ejectment Petitions
